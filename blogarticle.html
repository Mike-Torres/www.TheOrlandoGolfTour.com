Death Matches that Need to take place in 2019

January 2019


Tim Smyth Vs Mike "Commish" Totten

I think this match can go either way. Tim has been working in the off season and getting a lot of rounds in at Grand Cypress. Totten took the initial victory between these two but I think if caddies come into play, I think Tim gets a large advantage having his brother caddying. Totten would most likely go with Aaron Horne because he is a great calming factor on the course or Eli Roe because of his insane putting skills. This is an under the radar death match that I would definitely like to see.

Jerrod Paul Vs Mike "The Mouth" Torres

JP took down "The Mouth" the last time they played on a brand new course that Torres never saw before. When we asked Torres about the round his response: "This was a setup! This guy played me on a course that he knew". Torres sounded very upset after the interview and is looking to hunt down JP for a death match. Going into this match, JP would probably be the favorite.

Irishman Vs Frenchman

This is a match I'd sell blood to watch. These are two of top players on tour that have both work/worked in the golf indsutry and have much respect for the game. Both of them are scratch golfers and either one could win. Ross has a stellar all around game where if he starts pin seeking Rob could be in trouble. On the other hand, Rob plays on momentum and if he's dialed in he is almost impossible to beat because of his short game and especially is putting.
