What Courses do we play for our Majors

We make sure we vet that the course is top notch

Royal St. Cloud

5310 Michigan Avenue
St. Cloud, FL 34769
(407) 891-7010

With 27 holes across three courses, Royal St. Cloud will help you pull off a well-executed outing for any size.

Whether you’re looking to host a private golf clinic for a small group or a full-scale tournament for hundreds, we’re here to help you with every detail including banquet and catering, promotional items, signage, and high-quality tournament prizes.

We’re experts at making sure your guests are well cared for, and we’re confident we’ll exceed your expectations.

Orange County National

16301 Phil Ritson Way
Winter Garden, FL 34787
(407)656-2626

If you are looking for the best golf in Orlando, FL and want to know who has the finest course layouts, conditions and facilities, look no further than Orange County National Golf Center and Lodge.

Whatever the goal for your Orlando golf vacation – 36 holes per day, first class instruction, hours of range time, or memories of sharing good times with friends, OCN has something for you.

Two championship courses, a 9 hole short course, Central Florida’s Premier Golf Academy.

Grand Cypress

1 North Jacaranda Street
Orlando, FL 32836
(407) 239-1909

A truly diverse selection of Orlando golf courses. The nine hole North, South and East Courses offer a variety of mental and physical stimuli that, unlike other Orlando golf courses, test accuracy rather than strength and distance.

Although the North, South and East Nines have their own distinct qualities, they compliment each other beautifully to make three different 18 hole combinations. The North, South and East Nines offer a variety of tee boxes making the courses enjoyable for top level golfers and beginners.

Champions Gate

8575 White Shark Blvd
Davenport, FL 33896
(407) 787-4653

​ChampionsGate Golf Resort is a Central Florida golf masterpiece – when on the golf course it seems that you are miles away from civilization,and yet the Orlando Golf Resort is less than 15 minutes from Walt Disney World.

Together, the National and International golf courses bring a unique combination to Orlando Golf. Two totally different Greg Norman signature designs married with Florida’s natural habitat, with every hole featuring beautiful natural vistas. It's Orlando’s best golf outing and tournament venue.
