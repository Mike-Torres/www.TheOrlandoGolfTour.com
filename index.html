Welcome to the Orlando Golf Tour


What is the Orland Golf Tour

WE ARE A GOLF TOUR WITHIN THE ORLANDO FLORIDA AREA THAT PLAYS FOR MONEY AND PLAYS FOR THE TITLE OF CHAMPION

Talent Level

We play with all skill levels of player from scratch golfers to players with 20+ handicaps.

Where We Play

We play main events within the Orlando area. We do however go on trips to play in other parts of the country.

What's the Cost

There is no cost to play within this league. We do play for money and points so be ready to bring your A+ game.

Death Matches

This is the bread and butter of our league. This is a one on one match play for the set amount of money no less than $100.

How can I Join

This league is based on recommendations. We play with people that we like so we do vet before we let you in.

Game Modes

Depending on the size of group we will play games called "Wolf" "Best Ball" "Points Game" and "Scramble".

Meet The Founders

Mike Totten
Founder and Owner

Mike's Story
Mike was one of the original founders that created this league five years ago. He sets up all logistics to point calculations as well as creating updated rankings to all players.

He wanted to create a league with a group of friends that ended up spreading like a wild fire. He now organizes this league of over 100+ members.

Eli Roe
Founder and Champion

Eli's Story
Eli was a big inspiration on the league as he and a few other NBA, NCAA, high scool referees wanted to set up a golf league specifically to for them.

Eli is the mathematician of the league. Our events are made fair to play because of Eli's pre-written mathematic formulas. Eli Roe has a masters degree in mathematics.

Jason Carroll
Founder and Champion

Jason's Story
Jason Carroll is an NCAA and high school official. He is one of the premier referees in Orlando. His last game that he did up to date was the Camping World Bowl as the lines crew.

He is one of the founding fathers to the league. He won the Orlando Golf Tour Championship in 2013. He does live press interviews on the course with players.

Kent Mullens
Founder and Champion

Kent's Story
Known as the "Champ" or the face that run's the place, Kent Mullens was one of the founding fathers of this league.

Kent is very active in the Orlando community mentoring kids in sports as well as being part of the referee crew that originally started this league. He won the Championship in 2016 and brought the evolution of the "Belt".
